Who We Are

We are a collaboration of midwives passionate about family-centered care. With years of experience, we understand the enormity of birth and early parenting. We support informed choice without judgement, recognising everyone's individuality and respecting your decisions.

We recognise the physical toll and exhaustion of those first days, weeks, or months. Whether you're a first-time parent, growing your family, or a grandparent refreshing skills, we're here to support your transition with evidence-based care and practical assistance.

Our Service Packages

We provide personalised care tailored to your specific needs. Our packages are flexible and can be customised to serve you best.

While You Were Out

Support during your hospital stay with home preparation, pet care, and nursery setup. Ideal for those rushed to hospital or needing help with household tasks.

We're Coming Home

Prepare for your homecoming with ready meals, fresh linens, and someone there to help you settle in while you rest and recover.

Overnight Care

Experienced night support to follow your feeding plan while you catch up on much-needed sleep. Popular worldwide for new families.

Assistance with travel plans, whether it's a long-haul flight, road trip, or just a night away for a celebration.

Common Questions

We understand you may have questions about our services. Here are answers to some common queries:

When should I book your services?

We understand that babies rarely follow a schedule! When you book with us, we take into account that your baby might arrive earlier or later than expected. We'll work with you to create a flexible plan that can adapt to your needs.

How do your services work?

We come to you when you need us. Whether you want support for the first few days at home, overnight care, or assistance when your partner returns to work, we tailor our services to fit your specific situation.

What if I need additional support?

If you need a peaceful transition before returning home, we can also help with referrals to other excellent local services like lactation consultants, accomodation, exercise providers and photographers.

Do you offer emotional support for birth trauma?

Yes, we have qualified counsellors who can help you work through birth trauma or grief. We understand that birth can be a significant emotional event for parents and support people alike.
